Platform

Synopsis

Survive as long as you can in the Die Jail!

Tags

Dice - FPS - Game Maker's Toolkit Jam

Get game!

Game description

Die Jail is a first-person shooter where you are inside a die and fight against waves of enemies while the die rolls and changes its faces.
Survive long enough and you will encounter the Final Boss. Are you skillful enough to defeat all your enemies and escape the Die Jail?


Other information

Game made for GMTK Game Jam 2022.


Minimum requirements

  • Windows 10
  • GTX 970
  • Intel core i7
  • 8GB RAM
