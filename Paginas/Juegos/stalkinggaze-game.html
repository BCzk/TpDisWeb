Game description

Stalking Gaze is an atmospheric horror walking simulator where the player gets to play as Leonard Cornell, former actor that suffers from scopophobia. Through the game, the player will explore the protagonist's psyche, experimenting his phobia and his distorted perception of reality.


Other information

This game contains reference to sensitive subjects, such as suicide, paranoia, mental illness, etc. Player discretion is advised.
