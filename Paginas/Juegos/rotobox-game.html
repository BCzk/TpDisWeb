Game description

Rotobox is a puzzle game where you have to move boxes by moving your mobile device in the correct directions in order to get them to the goal.
Avoid getting any of the boxes in the level destroyed and finish quickly and efficiently to collect all the stars!


Other information

This game is in development and will be soon published to Google Play. Some of the Google Play specific functions may not work correctly.
