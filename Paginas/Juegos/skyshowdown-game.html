Platform

Synopsis

The player will experience the life and decay of a young man obsessed with academic and labor success.

Tags

2D - Short - Singleplayer

Get game!

Game description

"Sky Showdown" is a 2D action-platforming game where the player takes control of Roger Nelson, a violent policeman, who must cross through different rooftops fighting terrorists.
The goal is to reach the end of each level and fight its bosses. The game is strongly influenced by 80's and 90's action movies such as: "Die Hard", "The Terminator", "Lethal Weapon", "Showdown in Little Tokyo", to name a few, and newer ones such as: "John Wick".

Controls:
Arrow Keys - Move
Space Bar - Jump
Z - Shoot
Left Shift - Dash
