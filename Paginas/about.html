About Us

About BRISA

We are a game developer and publisher who focuses mainly on creative, innovative and fun indie games. We partner with talented devs to bring their visions to life and share them with audiences all around the world.

Our History

BRISA was born when a bunch of university friends came together in order to make great games for their assignatures. After some time those same friends decided it was time to take the step into the industry as professionals.

Our Philosophy

At BRISA we are committed to publishing titles that are fun and meaningful. We care deeply about the games we publish, and we are dedicated to helping developers create experiences that are authentic, innovative, and true to their vision.
